The Old West Dinner-Show Adventure

Every Friday and Saturday

7 to 9 P.M.

The Old West Dinner Adventure proudly presents a bigger than life, over-the-top, interactive old time western show. The entertaining, lively and spicy (but totally wholesome) doings at The Old West Dinner Adventure is an experience for the entire family. Come meet Miss Ruby, Parley, our Sheriff and Andie along with our band, for a variety of old west entertainment.

 We can't have you watching a show while your hungry so we'be included Dutch oven dinner complete with biscuits, cornbread, pork or chicken, baked beans and potatoes topped off with mouth watering homemade deserts.

This is not just a dinner show, this is an adventure you'll want to have over and over again!

MEET OUR BANDS

DYER HIGHWAY

Tel Dyer and his sisters Mattie and Tiann are on their way to the top of the charts, but for now at Showtime Utah they will keep you spellbound as they sing and play their way into your heart. Don't let the ages fools you, close your eyes and you'd think you were listening to Brad Paisley when Tel sings for you. Their charm, wit and antics are sure to entertain the very young and old alike. Songs like "Grandfathers Clock", "Devil Went Down to Georgia", "Cowboys in Flipflops" and the ever popular "Ghost Rider's in the Sky" are just a few of the tunes you'll hear. Check our schedule page for the dates they can be seen at Showtime Utah.

SIZZLING STRINGS

The SIZZLING STRINGS - David and Sherrie Jensen and their seven children will be making their fiddles, guitar, string bass, mandalin and cajon sizzle as they play some favorite familiar tunes like "The Devil Went Down To Georgia", "Cripple Creek", "Wabash Cannonball" and even "Red Wing" along with a variety of music from Valvalti, Phatom of the Opera and John Denver. Their diversity and energy will keep you entertained, clapping your hands and even singing along as they draw you into their style of mixing the Old West with more current familiar tunes. For reservations call 801-636-9669

MAMA'S BOY'S

Mama's Boys continue their long lasting relationship with The Old West Dinner Adventure. When Scott and his sons come together they bring a whole new meaning to the saying "family fun!". Nathan's crazy fiddle antics, Spencer and his interesting humor, Taylor's just plain country charm at the age of mere 11 (watch out girls, he's going to be a real cutie!), Jon and his amazing voice and Matt and his bass all join their dad, Scott. to keep you laughing, yeehawing, clapping and singing as they take you from the old west music of Ghost Riders in the Sky all the way to the '70's with Scarborough Fair and Black Water. Add our town characters to the mix and you will have a night to remember for quite some time!

: Mama's Boys at Showtime Utah

 

Michael "Boots" Robinson

Meet Michael “Boots” Robinson – Michael has an amazing voice, country charm and plenty of talent to keep an audience entertained for hours.  Along with the old west music you’ll enjoy his humorous and insightful cowboy poetry.  He is also joined by our town character to bring you a night of boot stomping, knee slapping, grub eating fun.
