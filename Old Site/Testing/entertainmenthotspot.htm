Entertainment Hot Spot
10 P.M. to Midnight
Starts Friday May 2nd

Every Friday Showtime Utah will present Entertainment Hot Spot, where you will see great entertainment from the known and maybe not so known musicians of Utah.  Whether it’s a solo act or a band, vocalists or instrumentalists they will be at the Hot Spot on Friday nights.

Keep on eye on this show because you may even see some pretty hot talent coming to Utah to perform who can often be found on the international entertainment circuit as well.  The word is out and performers from all over the country want to come to our little corner of the world to entertain you!

 Entertainment as it’s best!
