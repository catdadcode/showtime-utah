Family Night Live
6:30 to 9 P.M.
Shows start on Monday May 4th

Showcasing the amazing children of Utah and some of the older folks as well!  With singing, dancing, child magicians and jugglers, a comedian, and even a touch of the Old West antics, your Monday nights will never be the same.  Then, just when you thought it couldn't get better, add the amazing Alex Boye' as the host for the evening. But don't let it end there, let us make your family night treats for you.  Our concession stand will be open for the best homemade root beer floats in frosted mugs you have ever had along with popcorn, nacho's, ice cream treats and lot of drinks.  With everything combined for one great evening your Monday nights will have everything you need to start the week off right.
